Lists

Markdown supports both ordered and unordered lists.

Unordered List

Use *, -, or + followed by a space.

  • First item
  • Second item
    • Sub-item indented with two spaces
  • Third item

Ordered List

Use numbers followed by a period and a space.

  1. First item
  2. Second item
  3. Third item
